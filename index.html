copsy.js

Copsy is an event-driven form validation plugin for jQuery. It is not bound to any specific tooltip plugin.

You can view the source or download it.

Why another validation plugin?

  • Minimalist and extendable
  • It is the validation mechanism only. No
  • Not bound to a tooltip engine. Plug your own.
